# Fake Login Portal
Sets up a wireless network with a captive portal, which users can connect to and be prompted to enter info requred by you, that goes straight into your SD card.

## How to set up:

1. Paste the 'portal.html' and 'autorun.ds' files in the root of your SD card.
2. Unplug your device from power and plug it back in.

## How to use:
_It runs automatically on boot, no further setup required_.
